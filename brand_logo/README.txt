NoCashFlow — Brand Mark / Publisher Logo
========================================
Mark: Fraunces "N /" monogram (same family as the existing favicon), built on
the brand's signature forward-slash device, with a restrained baseline rule.
All content sits inside a circular safe-zone so Google News / Search circle-crops
do not clip it. Strokes are a heavier Fraunces optical cut than the old favicon —
chosen deliberately so the mark survives downscaling to 24-32px.

Baked background color: oxide-red  #B0442B
Mark / wordmark cream:             #F3EEE3
Wordmark ink (lockup):             #16181C
Color space: sRGB.
