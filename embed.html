Widget Free · No sign-up

Put live market data on your site

A free, self-contained widget — Crypto Fear & Greed, Bitcoin, gold and the US 10-year, refreshed daily from primary sources. One line of HTML, no API key, no tracking of your visitors.

1 · Script (recommended)

Paste this where you want the widget to appear. Works on any HTML page — WordPress (Custom HTML block), Ghost, plain sites.

<script src="https://nocashflow.net/embed.js" data-ncf-widget async></script>

Want it in a specific spot? Add a target: <div id="ncf"></div> then load with data-ncf-target="ncf".

2 · iframe

If your platform blocks scripts (some CMS post editors do), use the iframe instead.

<iframe src="https://nocashflow.net/widget/" width="380" height="340"
        style="border:0;overflow:hidden" loading="lazy"
        title="NoCashFlow live market data"></iframe>
The fine print

It's free to use, including commercially. The only ask: keep the “nocashflow.net” link intact — that link is how the widget stays free. The widget loads no third-party trackers and sets no cookies on your visitors. Data is a daily snapshot, provided as-is for information only, not investment advice.
