Bitcoin Funding Rate Today

The live BTC perpetual funding rate (Deribit) — what leverage is paying right now.

+0.0014%
Longs pay

Funding rate is the periodic payment exchanged between long and short positions on perpetual futures, keeping the contract tethered to spot.

Positive funding means longs pay shorts — leveraged traders are net bullish; negative means shorts pay longs. Persistently high funding often precedes long squeezes. Source: Deribit BTC-PERPETUAL (8h).
