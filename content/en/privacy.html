Privacy Policy

How NoCashFlow handles your data — in line with the EU GDPR.

Who is responsible

The data controller is Orkun Biçen, resident in Spain, operating the site nocashflow.net. Contact: orkun@nocashflow.net.

What we collect

If you subscribe to the bulletin, we collect your email address — nothing else. We do not ask for your name, and we do not build a profile on you.

Why, and on what legal basis

Your email is used solely to send you the bulletin. The legal basis is your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), given when you subscribe. You can withdraw it at any time — every email has a one-click unsubscribe link.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first measurement tool that uses no cookies, does not fingerprint you and does not track you across other sites. It reports only aggregate, anonymous traffic (page views, referrers). It cannot identify you.

What we never do

We never sell, rent or share your data. There is no advertising and there are no third-party advertising or tracking pixels on this site.

How long we keep it

We keep your email only for as long as you stay subscribed. When you unsubscribe, it is removed.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict and port your data, and to object to or withdraw consent for its processing. To exercise any of these, email orkun@nocashflow.net. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD).
